Calaguas Island

The Calaguas is 7.7 kilometers in diameter and located off the coast of Lagos, Nigeria. This is the perfect romantic getaway spot for couples and newly weds. The coast is home to some of the rarest aquatic life species and a breathtaking sunset scape and awesome boat cruises and dinners, you cannt go wrong with The Calaguas.
